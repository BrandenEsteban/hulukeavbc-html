ABOUT US

What Does Hulukea Mean?

Hulukea is a shortened version of the middle name of Club Director and Coach, Branden Lee Koa'ehulukea Esteban! Koa'ehulukea is a Long White-tailed Tropicbird that is indigenous to the Hawaiian Islands. This Volleyball Club will help your child to SOAR in the art of volleyball, excel in teamwork, and self confidence! The Hulukea Youth Volleyball Club is a Nonprofit Organization that benefits your 10-14 year old! Let's FLY!

OUR ELITE COACHING STAFF

Branden Esteban

Club Director and Head Coach of the Hulukea Youth VBC. Branden has always had a strong love and passion for Volleyball. From being the Ace of his 6th grade elementary team to the starting setter for the University of Hawai'i, Branden has many years of experience under his belt and wants to share his knowledge and wisdom to the youth of Hawai'i.

Kala Fuentes

Assistant Head Coach of the Hulukea Youth VBC. Like Branden, Kala learned how to play volleyball at a very young age. Kala had the privilege to attend a very prestigious school in Japan that excels in volleyball, Karasuno High School. He honed his skill in Japan, when he moved back to the islands, Kala landed the starting Outside Hitter position at the University of Hawai'i. Kala eagerly looks forward to spreading the love for volleyball to the next generation.

Maryvic Magsanoc

Assistant Head Coach and Massage Therapist of the Hulukea Youth VBC. Maryvic is a Licensed Massage Therapist and has been in the field for over 5 years. She had the privilege to play as the starting Setter for the University of Hawai'i Women's Volleyball team. After graduating from the University of Hawai'i, she became the Massage Therapist for the UH Athletics Department, with her expertise in Sports Therapy.

THE CLINIC

Clinic Information:

The Hulukea Youth Volleyball Club conducts clinics on Sundays, 3:30-5:30 p.m. at Ewa Mahiko Park, 91-1205 Renton Road in Ewa Beach. The volleyball clinic happens year round except for certain holiday weekends and the month of December.

Clinic Schedule:

3:15 p.m. - Sign in and family free play

3:45 p.m. - Warm up and agility drills

4:00 p.m. - Volleyball skills session

5:15 p.m. - Game play

5:30 p.m. - Clinic ends
